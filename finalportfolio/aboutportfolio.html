About Me!

Bio

Hi everyone! My name is Rafael. I am studying at Illinois State University majoring in Creative Technologies with an emphasis on Game Design. This will actually be my second bachelor's degree, as I have obtained my first one at ISU, studying music from Fall of 2016 to Spring of 2020. I'm excited to work in the game industry in anyway I can and gather experience to hopefully create many games of my own. I like the developing/coding side of things, it's fun figuring out how to code mechanics and all things and having it work!

Hobbies/Interests

My hobbies include video gaming (obviously), hanging with my friends on discord, playing my guitar, watching tv, eating fried chicken, or just eating in general. I'd love to sing in a choir again, or perform music in any sort of way. I picked up photography a while back and every now and then I go out and take some pictures.

I also like traveling places with friends, there's a lot to see and do in the world and I want to experience it all!
