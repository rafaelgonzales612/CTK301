Music Experience

I didn't start getting into the music world in general until 6th grade when I started band. I played trumpet for about 8 years. Then I joined my high school choir my sophomore year, and stayed until I finished my first degree at Illinois State University May 2020. I am classically trained vocally, being part of ISU's curricular choirs such as Men's Glee, Concert Choir, and Madrigals. I was also in one of ISU's four a cappella groups, the Clef Hangers, leading them as Music Director for a year.
